About Care Circle

Care Circle is a prototype community childcare platform built by the ByteBakers team. It focuses on helping parents in the Seattle, Bellevue, Kirkland, and Redmond area find trustworthy nannies and family-friendly locations.

How it works

  1. Search

    Browse nanny profiles and local activities from the navigation menu.

  2. Connect

    Open a nanny profile and review experience, availability, and skills.

  3. Meet

    Use the form flow to share your needs and request an introductory call.

  4. Book

    Confirm details directly with the nanny and use Care Circle as a hub for future care.

Who we are designing for

  • Parents like Milli (42)

    Recently moved to Bellevue and needs reliable childcare while adjusting to a new city.

  • Nannies like Amina (23)

    Student caregiver seeking part-time childcare jobs that fit her class schedule.

  • Local businesses like Jessica’s café

    Wants to let families know that kids are welcome, and share details about play areas and menus.

Accessibility (ADA) plan

  • Use clear headings and logical reading order.
  • Maintain readable font sizes (minimum of 16px in CSS).
  • Provide alt text for all non-decorative images.
  • Ensure keyboard-accessible navigation and visible focus states.
  • Use color combinations that follow WCAG contrast guidelines.
  • Write link text that explains the destination, not just “click here”.

This prototype is still in progress. If you notice an accessibility issue, please share it through our feedback form.
